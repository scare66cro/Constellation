Digital Inputs (FC02 / inject FC06)

Equipment inputs 0–9 are post-optocoupler (PS2504-4). On the real board a closed field contact = proving running, and the opto inverts it to a LOW at the microcontroller. This panel writes the chip-level DI, so PROVING injects DI low (closed contact); FAULT = contact open = DI high. (That inversion is why "closing proves running" looks backwards as a bit. E-Stop / DC24V are active-high: OK = high.) Bay lights are current-sense, shown as ON/OFF — DI high = lamp drawing current = ON.
